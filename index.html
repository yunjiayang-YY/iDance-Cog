Practice tips

  • Start with a slow tempo, then increase gradually.
  • Practice seated or standing near a stable support.
  • Say the movement name aloud for extra cognitive challenge.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • Space: start or stop practice
  • R: randomize the four boxes
